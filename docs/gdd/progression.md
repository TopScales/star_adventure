# Progression

Player loses when the ship is hit without an active shield.

Progression is level-based within a single run.

No meta-progression in MVP.

FTUE Level 1 – Movement & Momentum

Goal: Teach ship inertia and basic control.

No enemies.

Large, slow-moving asteroids.

Player must move through open gaps.

Subtle on-screen prompt: “Your ship has momentum.”

Success Condition: Reach the end of the short level.

FTUE Level 2 – Directional Shields

Goal: Teach shield orientation.

Single enemy firing slow projectiles from the front.

Front shield absorbs the first hit.

Visual break effect highlights shield loss.

Brief prompt: “Protect your sides.”

Success Condition: Survive until the enemy is destroyed.

FTUE Level 3 – Shields + Movement

Goal: Combine both core mechanics.

Enemies fire from alternating sides.

Player must rotate and reposition to keep active shields facing threats.

No hazards yet.

Success Condition: Complete the level.

FTUE Level 4 – Full Core Loop

Goal: Validate understanding.

Introduces environmental hazards.

No prompts.

Plays like a standard short level.

Success Condition: Complete or fail naturally.
